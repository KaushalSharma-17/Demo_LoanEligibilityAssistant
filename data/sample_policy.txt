----------------------------------------
1. PERSONAL LOAN POLICY
----------------------------------------
Minimum Monthly Salary: ₹30,000
Maximum Loan Amount: ₹10,00,000 (10 Lakhs)
Minimum Age: 21 years
Maximum Age: 60 years
Minimum Credit Score: 700
Loan Tenure: 12 months to 60 months (1 to 5 years)
Interest Rate: 10.5% to 14% per annum (based on credit profile)
Processing Fee: 1% to 2% of loan amount
Prepayment Charges: 2% on outstanding principal after 6 months; nil after 24 months
Maximum Loan-to-Income Ratio: Loan amount must not exceed 10 times the monthly salary

Eligibility Criteria:
- Applicant must be a salaried or self-employed Indian resident.
- Minimum 1 year of continuous employment with current employer.
- No active defaults or write-offs in the last 3 years.
- Debt-to-income ratio must not exceed 50%.

----------------------------------------
2. HOME LOAN POLICY
----------------------------------------
Minimum Monthly Salary: ₹50,000
Maximum Loan Amount: ₹1,00,00,000 (1 Crore)
Minimum Age: 21 years
Maximum Age: 65 years (at loan maturity)
Minimum Credit Score: 720
Loan Tenure: Up to 30 years
Interest Rate: 8.5% to 11% per annum
Processing Fee: 0.5% to 1% of loan amount
Maximum Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio: 80% of property value
Down Payment Required: Minimum 20% of property value

Eligibility Criteria:
- Applicant must be a salaried employee or self-employed professional.
- Minimum 2 years of stable income history.
- Property must be approved by the bank's empanelled valuers.
- Co-applicant (spouse or parent) may be required for higher loan amounts.
- No active defaults in the last 5 years.

----------------------------------------
3. CREDIT SCORE GUIDELINES
----------------------------------------
Credit Score Range and Classification:

750 and above — EXCELLENT
- Eligible for all loan products.
- Best interest rates offered.
- Minimal documentation required.
- Fastest processing time (2–3 business days).

700 to 749 — GOOD
- Eligible for personal loans and home loans.
- Standard interest rates apply.
- Full documentation required.
- Processing time: 4–5 business days.

650 to 699 — FAIR
- Eligible for personal loans only with additional conditions.
- A guarantor or co-applicant may be required.
- Higher interest rates apply (towards upper band).
- Enhanced scrutiny on bank statements and income proof.
- Home loans not available in this range.

Below 650 — POOR / REJECTED
- Not eligible for any standard loan products.
- Applicant may apply for a secured loan against fixed deposits.
- Recommended to improve credit score before reapplying.
- Minimum waiting period: 6 months after credit score improvement.

Factors Affecting Credit Score:
- Payment history (35%)
- Credit utilization ratio (30%)
- Length of credit history (15%)
- Credit mix (10%)
- New credit inquiries (10%)

Tips to Improve Credit Score:
- Pay all EMIs and credit card bills on time.
- Keep credit utilization below 30%.
- Avoid applying for multiple loans simultaneously.
- Do not close old credit accounts unnecessarily.
- Resolve any outstanding dues or disputes with lenders.

----------------------------------------
4. REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
----------------------------------------

For Salaried Applicants:
- Aadhar Card (Identity and Address Proof)
- PAN Card (mandatory for all applicants)
- Last 3 months' salary slips
- Last 6 months' bank statements (primary salary account)
- Form 16 (last 2 years) or latest Income Tax Return
- Employment confirmation letter or offer letter
- Passport-size photographs (2 copies)

For Self-Employed Applicants:
- Aadhar Card
- PAN Card
- Last 2 years' Income Tax Returns with computation
- Last 2 years' audited balance sheet and profit & loss statements
- Last 6 months' bank statements (business and personal)
- Business registration certificate / GST registration
- Passport-size photographs (2 copies)

For Home Loans (Additional Documents):
- Sale agreement / allotment letter of the property
- Title deed and property chain documents
- Approved building plan from municipal authority
- No Objection Certificate (NOC) from builder/society
- Property tax receipts (last 3 years)
- Encumbrance certificate
